*Dutch Research Council (NWO) Perspectief project.*

This project connects scientific computing and machine learning with biofabrication and sustainable living materials in life sciences. Alexander Heinlein contributes to the AI-guided biofabrication toolbox within the consortium and supervises the associated postdoctoral project.
